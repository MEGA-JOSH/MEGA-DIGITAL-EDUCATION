Capstone Project

Duration: 8 hours

Objective

Apply data science concepts to a real-world project.

Project Ideas

  • Customer Segmentation: Use clustering techniques to segment customers based on purchasing behavior.
  • Predictive Analytics: Develop a predictive model for sales, stock prices, or customer churn.
  • Data Storytelling: Create a report and visualization for a public dataset (e.g., healthcare, finance, climate).
  • Text Analysis: Perform sentiment analysis on customer reviews or social media data.

Project Requirements

Regardless of the chosen project, you will need to:

  • Data Cleaning: Prepare your dataset by handling missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
  • Data Visualization: Create visualizations to present insights and trends from your data.
  • Feature Engineering: Select and engineer features to improve model performance.
  • Model Building: Develop and evaluate machine learning models appropriate for your problem.
  • Presentation: Present your findings and actionable insights in a clear and structured manner.

Suggested Tools and Technologies

You may use the following tools and technologies for your capstone project:

  • Python: Use libraries like pandas, NumPy, and scikit-learn for data manipulation and modeling.
  • Jupyter Notebooks: Document your process and share your results interactively.
  • Tableau or Matplotlib: Create visualizations to support your analysis.

Capstone Project Report

Your final deliverable should include a comprehensive report that outlines:

  • Project Title and Description: Clearly define your project and its objectives.
  • Data Sources: Specify the datasets used and any preprocessing steps taken.
  • Methodology: Explain the methods and algorithms applied to your data.
  • Results: Present your findings, supported by visualizations and statistical analysis.
  • Conclusion and Future Work: Summarize your results and suggest possible improvements or future directions.
